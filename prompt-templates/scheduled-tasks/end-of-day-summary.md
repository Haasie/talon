# End-of-day summary

Compile a brief recap of the day. Focus on what moved, what's still open, and what's coming tomorrow.

<!-- Why: Closing the loop at end of day catches forgotten follow-ups before they
     go stale overnight. The tomorrow preview means you don't get surprised by
     early meetings or tight schedules. -->

<!-- Schedule: weekdays at your usual end time, e.g. "0 18 * * 1-5" -->

## Steps

1. **What happened today**:
   - Meetings that occurred (check calendar for today's completed events)
   - Tickets that changed status (assigned to you, updated today)
   - PRs merged or reviewed
   - Emails sent or flagged for follow-up

2. **Still open**:
   - Action items from today's meetings (check notes for anything captured)
   - Tickets in progress but not completed
   - Emails that need a response

3. **Tomorrow preview**:
   - Quick glance at tomorrow's calendar
   - Flag early meetings or tight schedules
   - Any deadlines approaching this week

## Format

```
## End of day -- [Day, Date]

### Done today
- [Completed items, meetings attended, decisions made]

### Still open
- [Items requiring follow-up]

### Tomorrow
- [First meeting time, key events, heads up]
```
